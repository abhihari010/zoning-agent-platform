Home occupation standards restrict outside storage and hazardous materials, limit
home occupation floor area to 25% of the dwelling, allow one nonresident worker,
prohibit on-site advertising signs, and require traffic volumes consistent with
nearby dwellings without adding parking spaces during the home occupation.
